You fall into a mound of snow. Brushing yourself off, you turn around, and are struck by the magnificence of the Fountain's abode. It is a twisting spire of pure diamond.

With your head poking just barely out of the pile of snow, you assess the situation. Men and women wearing purple scarves and mittens, and gleaming silver armor appear to be guarding the outer perimeter of the spire.

You realize you now have the false memories of a shadow priest. A lifetime of preparation, practice and battle experience is at your fingertips.

  • Use fortitude, to enhance your healthiness
  • Use major shadow summoning, to create a massive shadow being, which can distract the guards
